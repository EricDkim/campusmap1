How To Navigate

  • This application does not require a step by step guide in order to opperate this web application.

  • Therefore, the steps below are in of no particular order

  • Click on any of the highlighted buildings to navigate to classrooms and facilities.
  • Click on the sidebar to see the list of student events that are present this week
  • For administrators and moderators who have access to the Excel sheet, please click on the Campus Event Spreadsheet on OneDrive button
  • Helpful links are present at the footer of the web applicaiton
